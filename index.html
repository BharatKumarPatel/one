About Our School

Established with a vision to provide quality education in Balotra

Welcome to Shanti Niketan School

Founded in 1995, Shanti Niketan School has been a beacon of educational excellence in Balotra, Rajasthan. Our institution is committed to providing a nurturing environment that fosters academic achievement, character development, and holistic growth.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a comprehensive curriculum, we prepare students to face the challenges of the modern world while instilling in them strong moral values and a sense of social responsibility.

Our mission is to create lifelong learners who are not only academically proficient but also compassionate, creative, and confident individuals ready to make a positive impact on society.

Admission Process

Admissions at Shanti Niketan School are open for the academic year 2023-24. We follow a transparent and merit-based admission process.

  1. Submit the online application form
  2. Attend the interaction session (if applicable)
  3. Complete the documentation process
  4. Pay the admission fees

For more details about the admission process, fee structure, and required documents, please contact our admission office.
